Internal Design Management Department

Tripple Seven Timber Tech maintains a uniquely structured Internal Design Management Department composed of skilled professionals in architecture, engineering, and interior design. This department is responsible for managing and coordinating the entire design process, including architectural drawings, cost estimations, and interior design planning. The professional staff provide leadership and oversight to ensure all design submittals are delivered on time and adhere strictly to applicable building codes and quality standards. Their responsibilities also include comprehensive design quality control, cross-disciplinary coordination, and value engineering, ensuring the integration of efficiency, compliance, and aesthetic excellence in every project.
