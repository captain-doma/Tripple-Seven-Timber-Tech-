Construction

At Tripple Seven Timber Tech, we are firm believers in the power of partnership. A collaborative spirit grounded in trust, mutual accountability, and proactive communication fuels every project we undertake.

By fostering an environment where each team member seeks to support one another, we enable genuine collaboration that drives efficiency, innovation, and exceptional results.

Coordination and Communication

Weekly project foremen meetings and specialty subcontractor coordination sessions ensure tight integration and alignment across all trades. We include subcontractor Project Managers in these meetings to reinforce resource commitment and operational readiness.

We maintain a dynamic 6-week look-ahead schedule, broken down by area, task, and duration, and always aligned with the master project schedule. At Tripple Seven Timber Tech, we believe face-to-face interaction is irreplaceable when it comes to aligning goals, resolving issues, and building strong working relationships. Our Superintendents and Project Managers are highly skilled at motivating teams and supporting each member’s success through strategic planning, hands-on leadership, and mutual respect.

Cost Control

Budget accountability lies with our dedicated Project Managers. They work closely with our clients — reviewing and managing budgets on a monthly basis, or more frequently if needed. Our in-house accounting department ensures transparency and timeliness in financial reporting, processes all subcontractor payments, and manages formal payment requests on behalf of the client.

Quality Assurance

Quality control is a core value at Tripple Seven Timber Tech and a shared responsibility among all team members. From design through final installation, we instill a culture of excellence and accountability. Each team member is expected to fully understand their responsibilities and take ownership of delivering the highest level of craftsmanship.

Project Safety

Every worker is thoroughly processed through our Safety Inspection Team before beginning work on site. Our officers have the full authority to halt any operation, issue citations, and remove individuals from the site when safety standards are not met. This strict enforcement empowers our team to act decisively in preventing incidents and protecting everyone on the job.

We believe that a safe site is a productive site, and our goal is always zero incidents. Through training, leadership, and accountability, Tripple Seven Timber Tech fosters a culture of safety where every worker takes ownership — not only for their own well-being but for the safety of the entire team.
