Corporate Safety Commitment

At Triple Seven Timber Tech, we believe that optimal project outcomes are achieved through unwavering safety standards. Our safety philosophy encompasses:

  • Project-Specific Safety Plans: Customized safety strategies for each project.
  • Continuous Training: Regular safety education for all personnel.
  • Equipment Safety: Routine inspections and maintenance of tools and machinery.
  • Daily Briefings: On-site safety meetings to address immediate concerns.
  • Subcontractor Integration: Ensuring all partners adhere to our safety protocols.
  • Incentivizing Safety: Recognizing and rewarding exemplary safety practices.
  • Strict Enforcement: Implementing disciplinary measures for safety violations.

Safety Program Highlights

  • Risk Assessments: Identifying and mitigating potential hazards.
  • Emergency Preparedness: Establishing clear protocols for emergencies.
  • Health Monitoring: Regular health checks and wellness programs.
  • Incident Reporting: Transparent processes for reporting and addressing incidents.

Safety Performance Metrics

  • Zero Loss Time Incidents: Maintaining a record of no workdays lost due to injuries.
  • Low Incident Rates: Consistently below industry averages.
  • Positive Safety Culture: High levels of safety awareness and proactive behavior among staff.
